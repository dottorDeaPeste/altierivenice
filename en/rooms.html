On each floor a different pastel color in pleasant, relaxing shades characterizes the spaces dedicated to Hotel Altieri's 26 rooms.

Single, double, double-bed, triple, and family rooms (four beds), ideal for both tourist and business stays, are decorated with tasteful elegance and enhanced by the best comforts available among the 3 star hotels in Mestre.

Some rooms offer a spacious balcony and charming view, while all have TV, direct dial telephone, air conditioning, private bathroom with a tub or shower, and a hairdryer.
